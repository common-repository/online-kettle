A Wordpress widget for promoting your Salvation Army Online Kettle campaign.

== Description ==

About
The Online Kettle plugin makes it easy to promote your Salvation Army Online Red Kettle campaign from your Wordpress blog or website.

Usage
The plugin makes it simple to insert an attractive widget with your custom URL to promote your Online red Kettle campaign page anywhere on your website or blog.

Information
Visit the Online Kettle plugin page for complete documentation , support, information and to request  new features for the plugin.

== Installation ==

From your WordPress dashboard

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'Online Kettle'
3. Activate Online Kettle from your Plugins page.
4. Go to  'Appearance > Widgets'
5. Select the Online Kettle widget.
6. Enter your donation page URL.

From WordPress.org

1. Download Online Kettle.
2. Upload the 'online-kettle' directory to your '/wp-content/plugins/' directory, using your favorite method (ftp, sftp, scp, etc...)
3. Activate Online Kettle from your Plugins page.
4. Go to  'Appearance > Widgets'
5. Select the Online Kettle widget.
6. Enter your donation page URL.
